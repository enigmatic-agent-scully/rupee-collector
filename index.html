The fairies want to play a game with you. They will pick a random item and tell you its price.

You have plenty of rupees, but you don't know their values!

When you click on each rupee, its value will be added to your payment amount.

Be careful! You will only be able to buy the item if your payment amount matches the item price EXACTLY.

If your payment amount exceeds the item price, you lose.

Don't worry, keep trying!
