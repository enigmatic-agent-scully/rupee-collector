rupees collector

You will be given the random price of an item at the start of the game.

There are four rupees below. By clicking on a rupee, you will add a specific amount of rupees to your total payment.

You win the game by matching your total payment to the item price. You lose the game if your payment goes above the price.

The value of each rupee is hidden from you until you click on it.

Each time the game starts, the game will change the values of each rupee.
